A new kind of wallet

Vault is an easy to use, hassle free Desktop Wallet coded by the open source developer community rule110.

Why choose Vault?

A simple and easy way to manage your NKN assets.

All in one place

Finally an app to manage your NKN wallets in one place. You are afraid of using web wallets? With NKNxVault your data stays where it should be - secure and reliable.

Community driven

We love NKN as much as you do! Vault gets updated regularly and is actively maintained by the rule110 team and the NKN community.

Ease of use

Vault is built by blockchain fans for blockchain fans. We allow you to use every function of the NKN blockchain within a single app.
